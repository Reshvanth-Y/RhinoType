<h1>RhinoType</h1>

RhinoType is a high-speed typing assessment tool designed for developers and enthusiasts who want to track their speed and accuracy in a clean, distraction-free environment.

This project is completely inspired by the aesthetic and functional excellence of Monkeytype.


<h2>Features:</h2>
Live Metrics: Track your WPM (Words Per Minute) and Accuracy percentage in real-time.

Dynamic Word Generation: A diverse pool of common English words ensures every session is unique.

Visual Feedback: Immediate color-coded feedback for correct (white) and incorrect (red) keystrokes.

Responsive Cursor: A smooth, animated blinking cursor that tracks your position across lines.

Results Overlay: Comprehensive post-game stats including your Session Best score and a "New Record" badge.


<h2>📊 How It Works</h2>
WPM Calculation: Calculated by taking the number of correctly typed words and normalizing them against the 30-second game timer.

Accuracy: Derived from total keystrokes vs. error keystrokes to give you a precise look at your typing precision.

Navigation: Use Backspace to correct errors in the current word or move back to the previous word to fix mistakes.
